About our website

About Pixel Pilots Welcome to Pixel Pilots, the official alumni networking platform of your college or universities . We believe that the bonds forged during college don't have to end with graduation. Our mission is to create a thriving, connected community of past and present students, where experiences are shared, opportunities are created, and lifelong friendships are nurtured.

Who We Are Pixel Pilots is a passionate team of students and alumni from SRM RMP, united by a common goal: to bridge the gap between the generations of students who have walked through our college's halls. With a blend of creativity, technology, and a deep understanding of what it means to be part of this unique community, we’ve crafted a platform that’s as dynamic and diverse as our alumni.

What We Do Our platform is designed to offer alumni a space to reconnect, collaborate, and grow. Whether you're looking to catch up with old friends, network with professionals in your field, mentor current students, or find new career opportunities, Pixel Pilots is here to make it happen.

- Network: Explore professional opportunities and build connections with alumni working in various industries.

Our Vision We envision a world where our alumni network transcends the limitations of time and geography, fostering a culture of collaboration, learning, and mutual support. Pixel Pilots is committed to making this vision a reality, ensuring that the spirit of your college or universities lives on long after graduation.
